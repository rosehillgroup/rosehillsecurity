Why Choose Anti-Ricochet Tiles?

Enhance range safety with advanced ballistic absorption technology

Ricochet Elimination

Absorbs projectiles on impact, preventing dangerous ricochets and bullet fragmentation that can harm shooters and damage facilities.

Lead Reduction

Significantly reduces airborne lead exposure by capturing projectiles and preventing lead dust dispersion in the shooting environment.

Quick Installation

Simple, fast installation without complex procedures. Tiles can be mounted on walls, ceilings, or floors with standard fixings.

100% Recycled

Manufactured from recycled rubber, supporting environmental sustainability while delivering superior ballistic performance.

Modular Design

Available in multiple sizes up to 1m x 1m with various depth options to suit different calibers and range requirements.

Noise Reduction

Helps lower noise levels in shooting environments by absorbing sound energy, creating a more comfortable training experience.
