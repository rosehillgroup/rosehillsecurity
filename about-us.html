About Rosehill Security

Innovation in Security & Ballistic Protection

We design and manufacture robust, rapidly deployable security and ballistic protection solutions that protect people, infrastructure, training facilities, and public spaces worldwide.

Made in the UK. Deployed Worldwide.

At Rosehill Security, we specialise in rapidly deployable perimeter protection systems and advanced ballistic solutions designed to defend against unauthorised access, vehicle-borne threats, and ballistic risks. Our solutions combine speed of deployment with long-term durability, making security accessible and effective for any environment.

All our products are manufactured in West Yorkshire from 100% recycled rubber, supporting sustainability goals while delivering proven performance. We work with governments, security contractors, rail operators, military organisations, law enforcement, and international partners to provide solutions that are quick to deploy, cost-effective, and maintenance-free.

From hostile vehicle mitigation and anti-trespass panels to FB6-certified ballistic blocks and anti-ricochet tiles, we deliver practical security and ballistic protection solutions that protect without the disruption and cost of traditional infrastructure.
