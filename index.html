Security Solutions

Comprehensive protection systems combining speed of deployment with long-term durability

Rapid Defender

Ideal for crowd protection at events, offering manual installation in minutes with integrated pedestrian and vehicle access portals.

Impakt Defender

IWA 14-1-certified solid-rubber barrier system engineered to protect critical infrastructure against hostile vehicle attacks. Rapid to deploy, built to last.

Anti-Trespass Panels

Robust panels developed with the rail industry to prevent unauthorised access. Quick to install, maintenance-free, and effective on any surface.

Ballistic Solutions

Advanced ballistic protection systems for high-risk environments and training facilities

Ballistic Blocks

FB6-certified modular barriers providing rifle-grade protection. Surface-mounted, stackable blocks ideal for rapid deployment without permanent works.

Ballistic Tiles

Anti-ricochet tiles designed to enhance safety in live-fire environments. Perfect for shooting ranges and training facilities, eliminating dangerous ricochets.

Linatex Target Backing

Premium rubber sheeting for target backing applications. Durable, self-healing material that extends target life while capturing projectiles safely.
