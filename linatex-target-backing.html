Why Choose Linatex Target Backing?

Advanced self-healing rubber technology for superior range safety and longevity

Self-Healing Properties

Unique rubber composition naturally closes bullet holes, maintaining protection integrity and minimizing visible damage over time.

Exceptional Strength

Superior tear resistance and durability ensure long-lasting performance even under high-volume shooting conditions.

Ricochet Reduction

Significantly reduces ricochet and airborne lead risks, creating a safer shooting environment for all range users.

Noise Reduction

Effectively dampens noise from shots and bullet strikes, contributing to a more comfortable shooting experience.

Range Safety Compliance

Meets stringent range safety standards while providing long-lasting performance regardless of ammunition type used.

Versatile Installation

Suitable for indoor and outdoor ranges, easily installed behind targets for comprehensive ballistic protection.
