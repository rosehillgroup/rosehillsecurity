Why Choose Ballistic Blocks?

When security can't wait for construction - deploy rifle-grade protection in hours, not months

FB6 Ballistic Rating

Independently certified to stop 5.56mm NATO SS109 and 7.62mm NATO rounds at 10 metres. One of the highest civilian ballistic ratings available.

Rapid Deployment

Surface-mounted installation in under 24 hours. No digging, no permits, no disruption to site operations during installation.

Modular System

Stackable blocks adapt to any layout. Build walls from 305mm to 2440mm height with corner pieces for complete perimeter protection.

Sustainable Design

Made from 100% recycled rubber tyres with polyurethane binding. Reusable across multiple sites supporting circular economy goals.

Heritage Friendly

Non-permanent installation ideal for listed buildings, leased properties, and sensitive locations where traditional security isn't permitted.

All-Weather Performance

Withstands -20°C to +60°C, UV stable, moisture resistant. Maintains ballistic integrity in coastal air, sand, dust, and chemicals.
